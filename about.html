About Arsenii

Senior Technical Program Manager

Career & Technology

Arsenii Samoilov is a Senior Technical Program Manager and Enterprise Portfolio Leader with 19+ years of experience leading enterprise-scale product, AI, growth, and governance portfolios across global technology organizations. He has been a strategic partner to executive leadership at Intuit, Atlassian, Adobe, Salesforce, Roku, and Apple—shaping portfolio priorities, operating models, and cross-functional execution.

His work spans AI integration (scaling Atlassian Intelligence to 60%+ adoption), compliance governance (96%+ on-time regulatory closure at Intuit), monetization strategy ($50M+ annualized revenue delivered), and enterprise data modernization. He holds Product School, Certified Scrum Master, and Software Product Management credentials, with PMP certification in progress.

Community & Volunteer

Arsenii volunteers teaching kids in San Jose schools how to cook, helping young people learn practical skills and healthy eating. He also coaches 8-year-old hockey, helping young players develop skills and a love for the game.
